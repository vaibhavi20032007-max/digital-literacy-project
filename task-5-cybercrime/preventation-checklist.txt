🛡️ Stay Safe Online Checklist:
Never scan a QR code to receive money
Do not share UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone
Always verify payment requests before accepting
Avoid clicking on unknown or suspicious links
Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication
Install apps only from trusted sources like Play Store
Regularly check bank statements for unusual activity
Do not respond to unknown calls asking for financial details
💰 UPI-Specific Safety:
Remember: You pay money when entering UPI PIN, not receive
Double-check the receiver’s name before sending money
🚨 Report Cybercrime:
Website: cybercrime.gov.in
Helpline: 1930
